GAFIME (GPU-Accelerated Feature Interaction Mining Engine) is a diagnostic
engine for tabular datasets. It evaluates feature-target relationships with
multiple metrics, searches low-order interactions, and reports whether
learnable signal exists without training deep models.

The project supports CPU execution and a CUDA backend (via CuPy), with
optional backends for ROCm (PyTorch build) and MLX on supported platforms.
Compute costs are controlled by explicit budgets (combination limits and
VRAM usage settings) to keep analysis safe and predictable.
